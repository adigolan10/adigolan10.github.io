Eden Cinema

Final project, in progress

Adaptive Reuse|Cultural|Tel Aviv|Historic landmark|In progress
Overview

Developed as my final graduation project, Eden Cinema explores the adaptive reuse of one of Tel Aviv's most significant cultural landmarks. The proposal reintroduces the historic building as a vibrant public destination, combining preservation with contemporary cultural and community-oriented programs. Through workspaces, exhibitions, performances, screenings, and social gathering spaces, it aims to create an environment that evolves throughout the day while honouring the site's rich historical legacy.

Project currently in development. More to come.
